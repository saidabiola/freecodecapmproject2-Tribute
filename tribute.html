Prof. Albert Einstein

The greatest scientist ever lived.

Einstein's Biography

  • 1879 Born on March 14 at Ulm, Germany.
  • 1896 they moved to Italy and Albert continued his education at Aarau, Switzerland. He entered the Swiss Federal Polytechnic School in Zurich to be trained as a teacher in physics and mathematics
  • 1903 He married Mileva Maric in 1903 and they had a daughter and two sons; their marriage was dissolved in 1919 and in the same year he married his cousin, Elsa Löwenthal, who died in 1936.
  • 1905 He obtained his doctor’s degree. During his stay at the Patent Office, and in his spare time, he produced much of his remarkable work.
  • 1909 He became Professor Extraordinary at Zurich.
  • 1940 He became a United States citizen in 1940 and retired from his post in 1945.
  • 1955 He died on April 18, 1955 at Princeton, New Jersey.
